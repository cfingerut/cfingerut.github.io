Latitude vs. Humidity Comparison

Euclid of Alexandria--not to be confused with that of Megara-- once called it "the plight of reasoned thought". Tim Curry famously opined "it is a cruel gelatin". Queen Nefertiti once proclaimed it " as far from principled reason as Erriapus". What is this chastized object which draws the ire of the powerful and wise? You guessed it, humidity. More specifically, its relationship with latitude. Humidity was discovered 68 million years ago by our long lost friends, the dinosaurs (shortly before their demise 65 million years ago, that is 3 million years after their discovery). Humidity is theorized to be of the underworld and has haunted many humans. Some call it a curse, some call it a gift to remind us that not all sweat equally. Whether or not you agree with its intentions, it seems like humidity is here to stay.
